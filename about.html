We build furniture for homes that don't have room to compromise

Born from a real need

freeinch began in 2019 when co-founders Ananya and Dev couldn't fit a standard sofa into their home. They measured every wall, every door swing, every inch — and still came up short.

So they built their own. Friends asked for copies. Then friends of friends. Today we design and manufacture compact furniture for anyone who refuses to choose between style and space.

We're not a marketplace. We're a design studio with a factory — prototyping in real homes across Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi NCR, Pune, and Hyderabad before anything ships nationwide.

What we stand for

Precision over excess

We design to the centimetre. If a piece can't fit a standard Indian door frame or elevator, we redesign it — not your home.

Honest materials

Solid wood where it matters, engineered panels where it doesn't. No veneer pretending to be teak. Full spec sheets on every product page.

Installation included

Our teams mount, level, and haul away packaging. You shouldn't need a contractor for a desk — and with us, you won't.
