How it works

From first measurement to final bolt — a transparent process built for busy urban homeowners.

Measure

Book a free 30-minute home visit or send floor plans via WhatsApp. We verify door widths, ceiling heights, and elevator sizes.

Configure

Choose finishes and modular options. Receive a fixed quote — no hidden GST surprises, no post-delivery add-ons.

Deliver

Metro orders arrive in 5–10 business days. Flat-pack where needed; pre-assembled where possible. Real-time tracking link included.

Install

Our in-house team mounts, levels, and stress-tests every moving part. We haul packaging away. You just sign off.
